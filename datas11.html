Capstone Project

Apply all the data science skills you've acquired throughout the course to tackle a substantial real-world problem. Choose a dataset, perform analysis, build predictive models, visualize your findings, and present your results.

Project Ideas (Examples)

  • Predicting house prices based on features.
  • Classifying customer churn for a subscription service.
  • Analyzing sentiment from product reviews.
  • Clustering customers based on purchasing behavior.
  • Analyzing trends in public health data.

You will receive detailed guidelines, suggested datasets, and mentorship support to complete your capstone project.
